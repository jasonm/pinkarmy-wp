Welcome to Pink Army

Pink Army aims to make individualized breast cancer therapies using a revolutionary drug development pipeline. The goal? Safe, effective, and affordable cancer medicines in weeks, not years.

Who We Are

Pink Army is a drug development co-operative. We're bringing together scientists, foundations, and individuals to make innovative therapies faster and cheaper than ever before. It's biotechnology, open source.

What We Do

Each cancer is unique. Each treatment should be too. We're building a new, transparent drug development pipeline able to quickly translate diagnostic data into safe and effective drugs, custom made for one person at a time.
